# Support & Contact
Thank you for your interest in the TopSnipe Intelligence Platform.
TopSnipe Systems provides secure, mission‑focused data infrastructure for government, nonprofit, civic, health, environmental, and research organizations.

Because our platform operates in sensitive and regulated environments, support channels are structured to ensure security, privacy, and compliance.

## General Inquiries
For general questions about TopSnipe Systems, partnerships, or platform capabilities:
info@topsnipesystems.com

TopSnipe Systems collaborates with:

- Nonprofit organizations
- Foundations
- Public health agencies
- Environmental and resilience organizations
- Universities and research institutions

To discuss pilots, integrations, or regional data initiatives:
partners@topsnipesystems.com

## Technical Support (Authorized Users)
If you are an approved client, collaborator, or internal engineer:
- Refer to your onboarding documentation
- Use the secure support channels provided during implementation
- Follow internal escalation procedures for operational issues

For non‑urgent technical questions:
support@topsnipesystems.com

## Security Issues
If you believe you have found a security vulnerability:
- Do not open a public issue
- Do not disclose the vulnerability publicly

Instead, follow our responsible disclosure process:
security@topsnipesystems.com

See SECURITY.md for full details.
