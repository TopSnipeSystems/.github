# TopSnipe Systems — Code of Conduct
TopSnipe Systems builds secure, mission‑critical data infrastructure for government, nonprofit, civic, health, environmental, and research organizations.
We expect all interactions within this GitHub organization to reflect the professionalism, integrity, and community‑focused values of our work.

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All public and private repositories under the TopSnipeSystems organization
- All issues, discussions, pull requests, and documentation
- All contributors, partners, collaborators, and internal team members

## Our Standards
We strive to maintain an environment that is:
- Respectful — Treat all individuals with professionalism and courtesy
- Inclusive — Support diverse backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences
- Constructive — Provide helpful, actionable feedback
- Mission‑aligned — Prioritize community impact, security, and integrity
- Collaborative — Work together to strengthen public‑sector and nonprofit outcomes

## Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors are not tolerated:
- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Sharing sensitive or confidential information
- Posting security vulnerabilities publicly
- Disruptive or intentionally unproductive behavior
- Misrepresentation of identity or affiliation
- Attempts to access restricted systems or data
- Because TopSnipe Systems operates in sensitive sectors, any misuse of platform resources or data will result in immediate removal and may trigger further action.

## Reporting Concerns
If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:
- Do not open a public issue

Contact us directly at:
conduct@topsnipesystems.com

For security‑related concerns, follow the responsible disclosure process in SECURITY.md and contact:
security@topsnipesystems.com

All reports are handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.

## Enforcement
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
- Warnings
- Temporary or permanent removal from repositories
- Revocation of access to private systems
- Termination of partnership or collaboration agreements

TopSnipe Systems reserves the right to enforce this policy at its discretion to protect the integrity of the platform and the communities it serves.

## Our Commitment
TopSnipe Systems exists to strengthen community outcomes through secure, intelligent, and equitable data infrastructure.
We expect all interactions within this organization to reflect that mission.
