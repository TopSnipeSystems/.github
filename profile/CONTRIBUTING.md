# Contributing to TopSnipe Systems
Thank you for your interest in the TopSnipe Intelligence Platform.
At this time, contributions to this organization’s repositories are not open to the public.

TopSnipe Systems operates in mission‑critical environments across government, nonprofit, public health, environmental, and research sectors.
For this reason, all development, review, and deployment processes follow strict security, compliance, and governance standards.

## Security & Responsible Disclosure
- If you believe you have found a security vulnerability:
- Do not open a public issue
- Do not disclose the vulnerability publicly

Contact our security team directly at:
security@topsnipesystems.com

We will review all reports promptly and follow responsible disclosure practices.

## Partnership & Collaboration
TopSnipe Systems collaborates with:
- Municipal governments
- Nonprofit organizations
- Foundations
- Public health agencies
- Environmental and resilience organizations
- Universities and research institutions

If you represent an organization interested in partnership, data integration, or pilot programs, please contact:
partners@topsnipesystems.com

## Internal Contributors
If you are an internal engineer, contractor, or approved collaborator:
- Follow the engineering standards in shared-docs/
- Use the templates in shared-templates/
- Follow schema conventions in shared-schema-library/
- Commit messages must follow the internal commit standard
- All code must pass security and compliance checks before merging
- Access to internal documentation and tooling is provided through the TopSnipe Systems developer portal.
